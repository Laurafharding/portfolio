About Me

I am studying MSc Computational and Data Journalism Student at Cardiff University. I completed a BA (Hons) Journalism degree with a 2:1 from the University of Winchester in May 2016 with BJTC accrediation. Despite studying a broadcast journalism degree, my passions lie in data visualisation and investigative journalism. In 2016, I won a BJTC award for innovative student media.
